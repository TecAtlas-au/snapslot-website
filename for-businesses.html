The problem

An empty slot earns nothing.

A no-show at 2pm or a gap on a slow Tuesday can't be recovered later. SnapSlot gives you a way to recover that time at short notice, on your terms, at a price you choose.

How it works

List it, price it, get paid.

1

List an open slot

Got a gap today or this week? Post it in seconds with the service, time and duration.

2

Set your price

You choose the SnapSlot price. You're always in control of how much you offer.

3

Get booked

Nearby customers see your slot and book instantly. You're notified the moment it's filled.

4

Get paid out

Payments are collected at booking and paid to your bank via Stripe. No chasing, no cash handling.
