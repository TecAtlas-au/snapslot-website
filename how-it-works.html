How it works

Browse. Book. Show up.

SnapSlot connects you with last-minute openings at local businesses — often at a lower price, because the slot would otherwise go unfilled. Here's the whole flow.

For customers

Booked in under a minute.

1

Browse openings near you

Open the app to see last-minute slots from local businesses, each at its SnapSlot price.

2

Grab a slot

Found one that fits? Tap to book. The price you see is the price you pay.

3

Pay securely in-app

Checkout is handled securely by Stripe. SnapSlot never sees your card number.

4

Turn up & enjoy

Your booking details live in the app. Just arrive at the appointment time. Done.

Bookings are non-refundable. Slots are last-minute, so please only book what you can make.

Good to know

The common questions.

How is the price set?

The business sets the price to fill a genuine last-minute gap — you're seeing real openings from local businesses near you.

How do payments work?

You pay in the app at the time of booking. Payments are processed securely by Stripe.

What if I can't make it?

Because slots are last-minute and time-sensitive, bookings are non-refundable. Book with confidence.
